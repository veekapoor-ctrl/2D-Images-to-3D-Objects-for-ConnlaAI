This is the most beginner friendly method to convert 2D flat images to 3D. ImagetoSTL is a free open source online image converter.
Method does not require prior knowledge of programming languages but requires a basic understanding of depth, lighting and contrast. 

STEP BY STEP:
1. Open https://imagetostl.com/
2. Choose your conversion option on the ribbon at the top.
3. Upload your 2D Image
4. Select if you want your image coloured/B&W, etc and adjust dimentions.
5. The image is converted into a 3D object and can be viewed from all directions.
6. Adjust lighting, contrast, colour, etc according to your preference.
7. A basic 3D model is ready.

This model can be imported into Blender and you can repair and retouch the object according to preferences.
